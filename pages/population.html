Population

Population

As of 2023, Bhutan's population is approximately 770,000. This figure is an estimate, as detailed population surveys in Bhutan are conducted infrequently.

Ethnic Groups

Bhutan’s population can be divided into three major ethnic groups:

  1. Drukpa: The largest ethnic group, making up around 50% of the population.
  2. Lhotshampa: Comprising about 35% of the population, they are mainly of Nepalese origin.
  3. Indigenous Groups: Several smaller indigenous communities inhabit Bhutan.

Population Growth Rate

Bhutan's population growth rate is relatively low, at about 1.1% per year in 2023. The total fertility rate (TFR) is approximately 2.0-2.1 children per woman.

Health Situation in Bhutan

Health and sanitation in Bhutan have seen significant development over recent years.

Healthcare System

Bhutan has a developing healthcare system with hospitals and clinics in every district.

Personality of Bhutan

Bhutan has a unique personality and culture that reflects the beliefs and values important to its people.
